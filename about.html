About Us

Established in 1998, Cake Hut revolutionized the concept of the bakery chain in Karachi by introducing a culture of innovation in product development and providing easy access with branches in every neighborhood across the city. Over the last 20 years we have expanded to become the largest bakery retail network in the city and we are not done yet in fact, we are growing every day! Our mission is to give our customers the most authentic and artisanal bakery experience at the best price. All our products whether it is bread, biscuits, sandwiches, cakes or brownies are freshly baked every day using the best natural ingredients and time honored processes. We know our customers are deeply concerned about what goes into their food so we work hard to ensure that every batch is baked from scratch and is preservative and additive-free as far as possible.
